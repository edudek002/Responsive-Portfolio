About Me

I like traveling in the United States and in Europe. I particulary enjoy hiking in the mountains. Last summer I trekked tour du Mont Blanc, covering a distance of about 170km, including 10km of ascent and descent. In the winter I enjoy downhill skiing in various areas in Colorado, Utah, British Columbia and the Alps.

Three years ago, I founded a Girls Who Code program at the University of Illinois at Chicago. The goal of this program is to close a gender gap in Computer Science. I directed year-round activities for 60 middle and high school girls as well as two 7-week Summer Immersion Programs. This year I am leading an Intermediate level of the Girls Who Code program at the U of M.
